# 🚀 Idea Development Documentation

## 👥 Team Formation & Process

This team was created based on our shared interest in games. Since we
are working remotely, we connected through Discord and often played
games together as a way to collaborate and build ideas. This helped
shape the direction of our portfolio project.

After forming the team, we followed a structured process:

1.  Created a Google Docs file to list down our initial ideas.
2.  Moved to Notion to expand on those ideas, add more details, and
    begin voting on the best concept.
3.  During ideation, we also consulted AI tools (Gemini and ChatGPT) to
    get opinions, identify limitations, and explore possible
    improvements.
4.  Finalized our selected idea and summarized everything in this
    Markdown document.

## 💡 Other Ideas Considered

### Crime Prevention Social App

**Purpose:** A social app for Melbourne's at-risk public to share safety
information and receive alerts.

**Key Features:** SOS alert, GPS sharing, police upload

**Strengths:** Useful in situations where users cannot call emergency
services.\
**Weaknesses:** Privacy law concerns and permission issues with GPS
tracking.

**Target Audience:** Melbourne public, at-risk users

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Traveller Life Buddy

**Purpose:** Companion app for solo travellers focusing on safety.

**Key Features:** Cross-path alerts, travel timeline, matching

**Strengths:** Helps travellers connect and stay safe.\
**Weaknesses:** Real-time location sharing raises legal and safety
concerns.

**Target Audience:** Solo travellers, backpackers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### AI Plant Care Companion

**Purpose:** Helps users care for plants using AI guidance.

**Key Features:** AI ID scan, health diagnostics, reminders

**Strengths:** Solves common plant care knowledge gaps.\
**Weaknesses:** Risk of incorrect diagnosis due to poor image quality.

**Target Audience:** Plant owners, gift recipients

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Apartment Research Tool

**Purpose:** Aggregates government data into a single report for rental
decisions.

**Key Features:** Gov API aggregation, red flag alerts, comparison

**Strengths:** Centralizes scattered information.\
**Weaknesses:** Data inconsistency and legal risks (defamation, outdated
data).

**Target Audience:** Renters, interstate movers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### AI Grocery Assistant

**Purpose:** Helps users manage grocery shopping efficiently.

**Key Features:** Inventory tracking, smart lists, price comparison

**Strengths:** Reduces food waste and improves budgeting.\
**Weaknesses:** Limited API access in Australia and manual data entry
issues.

**Target Audience:** Families, students, budget shoppers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Purchasing Scam Detector

**Strengths:** Protects users from fraudulent listings.\
**Weaknesses:** AI reliability and detecting sophisticated fake reviews.

## ✅ Selected MVP: Fitness App Using Mediapipe

### 📌 Overview

We are building a fitness web application that uses Mediapipe to gamify
workouts. The app allows users to interact with games using body
movement tracked through their camera.

### 🎯 Rationale

Many people struggle with motivation when it comes to exercise. By
combining fitness with gaming, we introduce an element of engagement and
instant feedback, making workouts more enjoyable and consistent.

### ⚙️ Core Features

-   Movement tracking using Mediapipe\
-   Game-based workout interactions\
-   User profiles with XP, levels, and streaks\
-   Progress tracking and statistics

### 🌍 Potential Impact

This application aims to: - Help users build consistent fitness habits\
- Support individuals who rely on gamification and instant rewards
(e.g., neurodivergent users)\
- Make exercise more accessible and engaging

### 🔮 Future Enhancements

-   Badges and achievements\
-   Multiplayer / co-op gameplay\
-   Leaderboards\
-   Expanded game library

### ⚖️ Constraints

To stay within legal boundaries, only a limited number of simple and
distinct games will be implemented. The focus is on demonstrating the
technology and concept rather than building a full-scale game platform.
